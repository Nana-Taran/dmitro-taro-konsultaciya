# Dmitrii Summary

Dmitrii is a private tarot and esoteric consultant who works online in Ukrainian and Russian. He offers tarot consultations, situation diagnostics, relationship-related esoteric work, love magic, energy cleansing, protection, and money-scenario diagnostics.

The website frames the first contact as a short free diagnosis. Dmitrii does not guarantee outcomes, does not pressure visitors, and explains the format and cost before paid work starts.

Esoteric consultations do not replace medical, legal, psychological or financial help.
