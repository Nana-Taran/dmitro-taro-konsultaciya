# Dmitrii — Keyword And Google Ads Strategy

> Working strategy based on the reference Google Ads export and the approved premium website direction.
> Purpose: keep direct search demand without making the brand look like a cheap aggressive esoteric landing page.

---

## 1. Core Principle

We do not remove Dmitrii's real services.

Direct services such as `приворот`, `любовна магія`, `зняти порчу`, `зняття негативу`, `захист`, `грошова магія`, `обряди` and `Таро` must exist on the website because people search for them directly.

But we separate three layers:

1. **Google Ads layer**: softer, safer, built around diagnosis, online consultation, tarot and relationship questions.
2. **SEO layer**: direct pages for exact searched terms, written carefully and without manipulative promises.
3. **Brand layer**: premium, human, calm, trust-first presentation.

The site must be findable for direct intent, but the first impression must feel trustworthy and expensive.

---

## 2. Reference Campaign Verdict

The uploaded Google Ads export is useful as a demand map, not as a creative model.

What is useful:

- it proves direct demand exists;
- it shows hot search clusters;
- it separates Ukraine and diaspora geos;
- it contains many long-tail keywords;
- it includes retargeting and mobile-first thinking.

What we should not copy:

- aggressive copy such as "любимый человек вернётся";
- direct "приворот" and "порча" as the main ad face;
- "сильнейшая гадалка" / "потомственная" style;
- broad-heavy structure;
- mixed identities and domains;
- PMax with unrestricted final URL expansion;
- risky health/alcohol and urgent money keywords.

The campaign can work because the intent is hot. Our task is to keep the demand but improve trust, compliance and brand quality.

- Ukrainian tarot online
- Ukrainian esoteric consultation
- Relationship consultation online
- Tarot in Ukrainian abroad

Landing pages:

- `/online-konsultatsiia/`
- relevant `/geo/*.html` pages

### Campaign 3: SEO-Retargeting / PMax Later

Use only after:

- conversion tracking is working;
- clean landing page exists;
- assets are approved;
- risky direct SEO pages are excluded from final URL expansion if needed.

Rules:

- disable or restrict final URL expansion if Google may route traffic to risky pages;
- use clean images, not frightening occult visuals;
- avoid miracle claims in asset texts.

## 5. Ad Copy Direction

Safer ad headlines:

- Дмитрій — таролог онлайн
- Консультація Таро онлайн
- Почніть з діагностики
- Питання про стосунки?
- Приватна консультація
- Онлайн українською
- Конфіденційно та без тиску
- Розклад Таро на ситуацію

Safer descriptions:

- Напишіть Дмитрію у Telegram або Viber. Перша коротка діагностика безкоштовна.
- Онлайн-консультація щодо стосунків, Таро та особистих питань. Без тиску.
- Дмитрій пояснює, що бачить, і заздалегідь озвучує формат та вартість.
- Езотерична консультація не замінює медичну, юридичну чи фінансову допомогу.

Avoid in ads:

- Любимый человек вернётся
- Приворот на любовь
- Сильнейшая гадалка
- Не отчаивайтесь
- Помогу быстро
- Снять порчу срочно
- Обряд на деньги
- Консультации 24/7 if not true

## 6. Website Content Requirements From Keyword Strategy

The website must include:

- direct SEO pages for exact magical-service terms;
- a clean Google Ads landing page;
- a central online consultation page;
- a pricing / formats page;
- service FAQ blocks;
- AI-readable summaries;
- internal links between soft pages and direct SEO pages.

Example internal link flow:

- Home -> Стосунки -> Приворот / Любовна магія
- Home -> Очищення -> Зняття порчі / Захист

## 7. Measurement Requirements

The reference CSV does not contain performance statistics such as impressions, clicks, cost or conversions.

For real optimization, export a Google Ads report with:

- Campaign
- Ad group
- Keyword
- Search term
- Match type
- Impressions
- Clicks
- CTR
- Cost
- Conversions
- Cost / conversion
- Conversion rate
- Final URL
- Device
- Location

Do not judge final performance by structure export alone.
