## 4. Main Weaknesses

### Too Much Broad Match

Positive keywords:

- 2,456 broad;
- 44 exact.

This is too broad for a sensitive niche. It may work if the account already has conversion history, but it is risky for a new or premium brand.

### Aggressive And Risky Copy

Examples from the export:

- "Любимый человек вернётся"
- "Белые методы. Помогу быстро"
- "Не отчаивайтесь"
- "Сильнейшая гадалка"
- "Привороты На Девушку"
- "Снять порчу и сильный негатив"
- "Помогу снять венец одиночества"

These can create trust and moderation risks.

### Risky Keyword Clusters

Examples:

- навести порчу;
- порча на смерть;
- сильный приворот;
- привязка;
- ритуал на срочные деньги;
- заговор от алкоголизма;
- врачи-style adjacent claims if used later;
- вернуть любой ценой-style intent.

These are useful as market intelligence, but must be handled carefully.

### Brand Consistency Problems

The export mixes names and domains:

- Алена;
- Марія;
- Крстина / Кристина-style references;
- several domains and final URLs.

For Dmitrii this is unacceptable. The brand must be one real person with one consistent identity.

### PMax Risk

Performance Max has final URL expansion enabled in the reference campaign.

For Dmitrii, this should only be used later and carefully. If PMax is allowed to expand to direct SEO pages such as `pryvorot.html` or `znyattia-porchi.html`, moderation risk can increase.

## 6. What To Borrow For Dmitrii

Borrow:

- demand clusters;
- Ukraine / diaspora separation;
- mobile-first thinking;
- retargeting idea;
- direct SEO page necessity;
- long-tail FAQ ideas.

Do not borrow:

- aggressive claims;
- miracle promises;
- broad-heavy launch;
- mixed identities;
- "strongest fortune teller" style;
- fear-based copy;
- uncontrolled PMax URL expansion.

## 7. Recommendation For Dmitrii

Create a cleaner strategy:

1. Launch with Search campaigns first.
2. Separate Ukraine and diaspora.
3. Use phrase and exact match first.
4. Use safe ad copy around diagnosis and consultation.
5. Keep direct terms on SEO pages.
6. Build a dedicated `/ads/` landing page.
7. Build direct SEO pages for `приворот`, `любовна магія`, `зняти порчу`, `грошова магія`.
8. Track Telegram, Viber, WhatsApp clicks and form submits.
9. Add PMax only after conversion tracking and safe assets are ready.

---

## 8. Final Verdict

The reference campaign is probably commercially useful because it targets hot demand.

But for Dmitrii, it should be treated as a raw market map, not as a brand model.

The new project should keep the demand, upgrade the trust layer, reduce policy risk and make the site feel like a premium personal expert, not a generic aggressive esoteric ad funnel.
